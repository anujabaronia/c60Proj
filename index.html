About

My hobby is reading novels in my free time. Whenever I get time after completeing my daily work I like to read. I love the work of Mrs. Sudha Murthy. She writes simple and interesting stories. Her writing style keeps me engrossed, making me to continue reading. This hobby can be developed by anyone however I got this naturally. Reading books keep one always happy and busy. It is the good source of enjoyment, knowledge, inspiration and instruction. It makes us disciplined, loyal, punctual.
